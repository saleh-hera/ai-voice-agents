## What Nova Sales does
A voiced AI shopping assistant for online stores. She handles:
- 🛍️ **Order taking** — guides customers through purchases
- 📦 **Order tracking & shipment status**
- 🔄 **Returns and refunds**
- 🔎 **Product questions & recommendations**
- ⬆️ **Natural upselling** of complementary items

Trained on a store's full catalog, Nova gives every customer instant, accurate help — increasing conversion and cutting support load.

## The problem she solves
Shoppers abandon carts when they can't get a quick answer. Nova answers instantly, any hour, and nudges the sale forward.
