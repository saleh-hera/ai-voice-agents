## What Bella does
Bella is a fully-voiced AI receptionist for restaurants. She handles:
- 📅 **Reservations** — booking, modifying, and confirming tables
- 🥡 **Takeout & delivery orders**
- 🍝 **Menu, hours & dietary questions**
- 🎉 **Catering and event inquiries**
- 💬 **Complaint handling** with a warm, five-star-hospitality tone

She works 24/7, handles unlimited simultaneous calls, and never puts a guest on hold — so restaurants stop losing revenue to missed calls.

## The problem she solves
During the dinner rush, staff can't always answer the phone. Every missed call is a lost reservation or order. Bella catches every one.
